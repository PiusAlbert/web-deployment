About Us

This is what we do.

Welcome to Hoops Tanzania, your go-to source for all things basketball in Dar es Salaam and beyond! Our mission is to bring you the latest news, updates, and stories from the vibrant basketball scene across Tanzania. Whether it’s local league highlights, player profiles, or exciting tournament coverage, we’ve got you covered.

At Hoops Tanzania, we are passionate about showcasing the talent and dedication of our local athletes and teams. We believe in the power of basketball to unite communities and inspire the next generation of players. Join us as we celebrate the game we love and the incredible people who make it happen.

Stay tuned for in-depth articles, exclusive interviews, and live updates from courtside. Let’s grow the basketball community together!
